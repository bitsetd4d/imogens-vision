# Imogen’s Vision Website Specification

## Purpose

This document outlines the suggested content and structure for the **Imogen’s Vision** website.

The website should present Imogen’s Vision as a clear, professional and commercial proposition focused on motivational coaching, confidence-building, habit support and long-term lifestyle change.

---

## Key Points to Feature on the Website

### 1. Clear Headline

The site should quickly explain who Imogen is and what she helps people do.

Example headline options:

> Motivational coaching to help people build confidence, improve habits and move forward.

Or:

> Imogen’s Vision: practical motivation for lasting lifestyle change.

---

### 2. Who Imogen Is

A warm, personal introduction should explain who Imogen is and why people connect with her.

This section could cover:

- Imogen’s story
- Her personality, values and strengths
- Why people connect with her
- Her passion for motivation and helping people progress
- Her role as a positive, practical support figure

The tone should feel human, warm and authentic rather than corporate.

---

### 3. What Imogen Offers

The website should clearly explain the support available.

This could include:

- Motivational coaching
- Confidence-building support
- Habit-building guidance
- Encouragement and accountability
- Support with long-term lifestyle change
- Helping people stay focused between appointments or milestones

---

### 4. The Purpose of Imogen’s Vision

This section should explain the wider mission behind the work.

Key themes:

- Helping people believe change is possible
- Supporting realistic, long-term progress
- Encouraging confidence and self-belief
- Turning motivation into practical action
- Helping people keep going when progress feels difficult

---

### 5. Why Work With Imogen

This section should answer: **“Why Imogen?”**

Suggested points:

- Authentic and relatable
- Warm, positive and encouraging
- Focused on practical progress, not perfection
- Helps people feel supported and understood
- Encourages small, achievable steps
- Supports motivation over time, not just one-off inspiration

---

### 6. Imogen’s Message

The site should include a memorable message that captures the heart of Imogen’s Vision.

Example:

> You do not have to change everything overnight. Small steps, repeated consistently, can change your direction.

---

### 7. Photos and Media

The website should include visual content that feels professional, warm and approachable.

This could include:

- Strong, positive photos of Imogen
- Images that feel professional, warm and approachable
- A short introductory video, if possible
- Simple visual branding that feels personal but credible

---

### 8. Testimonials

When available, testimonials should be included to build trust and credibility.

Potential sources of feedback:

- Clients
- Patients
- Families
- Professional partners
- Organisations or clinics

Early on, this section can use placeholder wording such as:

> Client feedback coming soon.

---

### 9. How to Enquire or Book

The site needs a clear call to action.

Possible call-to-action wording:

- Book a consultation
- Make an enquiry
- Work with Imogen
- Contact us

The contact form should ask for:

- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Reason for enquiry
- Preferred contact method
- Message

---

### 10. Commercial Offer

Because this is a commercial proposition, the website should clearly explain how people can engage with the service.

This could include:

- Coaching packages
- Introductory consultation
- One-off motivational session
- Ongoing support package
- Clinic-based support
- Bespoke support for organisations or partners

Pricing does not need to be included on day one, but the offer should be clear enough that people understand what they are enquiring about.

---

### 11. Social Media Links

The website should make it easy for people to follow Imogen’s journey.

Potential channels:

- Instagram
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- YouTube or TikTok, if relevant

---

## Revised Website Structure

### 1. Home

**Purpose:** Quickly explain what Imogen’s Vision is and encourage enquiries.

Suggested sections:

- Hero headline
- Short introduction to Imogen
- What she helps with
- Why work with Imogen
- Main call to action

---

### 2. About Imogen

**Purpose:** Tell her story and build trust.

Suggested sections:

- Imogen’s background
- Her values and approach
- Why she does this work
- Her message

---

### 3. Coaching & Support

**Purpose:** Explain what she offers commercially.

Suggested sections:

- Motivational coaching
- Confidence-building support
- Habit and lifestyle support
- Accountability and encouragement
- One-off and ongoing support options

---

### 4. Work With Imogen

**Purpose:** Describe the commercial options.

Suggested sections:

- Introductory consultation
- Individual coaching
- Ongoing support packages
- Clinic or partner-based services
- Bespoke enquiries

---

### 5. Testimonials

**Purpose:** Provide social proof once available.

Suggested sections:

- Client feedback
- Partner feedback
- Outcomes or success stories
- Placeholder content until testimonials are ready

---

### 6. Contact

**Purpose:** Make enquiry simple.

Suggested sections:

- Contact form
- Email address
- Phone number, if appropriate
- Social links
- Clear enquiry button

---

## Recommended Starting Website

Start with a simple **5-page version**:

1. Home
2. About Imogen
3. Coaching & Support
4. Work With Imogen
5. Contact

Add a dedicated **Testimonials** page later once there is strong feedback to include.

---
