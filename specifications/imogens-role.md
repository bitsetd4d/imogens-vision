# Imogen’s Role

## Role title

**Founder and Motivational Coach**

## Simple description

Imogen is the founder of Imogen’s Vision and works as a motivational coach. Her role is to support, encourage and guide people as they build healthier habits, confidence and long-term lifestyle change.

## What Imogen does

Imogen’s role is to:

- explain the purpose and vision behind Imogen’s Vision
- support people in making realistic lifestyle changes
- help people stay motivated and encouraged over time
- build confidence through positive, practical support
- help people focus on progress, not perfection
- encourage healthy routines and better day-to-day habits
- work alongside the wider team, without replacing medical or clinical advice

## What this means in practice

In practice, Imogen helps people feel listened to, supported and motivated. She is there to encourage small, realistic steps that can be built into everyday life.

Her focus is not on quick fixes. It is about helping people make steady progress, build better habits and feel more confident in their ability to change.

## What Imogen is responsible for

Imogen is responsible for:

- representing the values of Imogen’s Vision
- helping shape the direction of the project
- supporting people in a positive and professional way
- encouraging commitment, consistency and self-belief
- helping people understand that long-term change takes time
- recognising when someone needs support from another member of the team

## What Imogen is not responsible for

Imogen’s role is not to diagnose, treat medical conditions, or give clinical advice.

Where someone needs medical, nutritional, psychological or specialist support, Imogen should help guide them towards the right person or service.
