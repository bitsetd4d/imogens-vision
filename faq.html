Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about working with Imogen.

What is motivational coaching?

Motivational coaching is about helping you find and maintain the drive to make positive changes in your life. It's practical, supportive and focused on realistic progress — not quick fixes.

Who is coaching suitable for?

Coaching is for anyone who wants to build confidence, improve their habits or make lasting lifestyle changes. You don't need to be in a crisis — many people come because they simply want support to move forward.

Is this a replacement for medical advice?

No. Imogen's Vision provides motivational coaching only. It does not replace medical, nutritional or psychological advice. Imogen works alongside clinical professionals and will always recommend specialist support where appropriate.

How do sessions work?

Sessions are relaxed, one-to-one conversations focused on your goals. They can take place in person or remotely, depending on your preference and circumstances. Each session is tailored to where you are and what you need.

How much does coaching cost?

Coaching options and pricing are discussed during your introductory consultation. Imogen offers flexible packages to suit different needs and budgets. Get in touch to find out more.

How do I get started?

Simply get in touch through the contact page. Imogen will arrange an introductory conversation to understand your goals and explain how she can help.

How long does coaching take?

That depends on you. Some people benefit from a few focused sessions, while others prefer ongoing support over several months. There's no fixed commitment — Imogen works at your pace.

Can Imogen work alongside my existing healthcare team?

Yes. Imogen regularly works alongside GPs, therapists and other professionals. Motivational coaching complements clinical care by providing encouragement, accountability and practical support between appointments.

Still Have Questions?

Get in touch and Imogen will be happy to help.
