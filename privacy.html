Privacy Policy

How Imogen's Vision collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Who We Are

This website is operated by Imogen's Vision. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@imogensvision.co.uk.

What Data We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address and message. We do not collect any data automatically beyond what is necessary to serve this website.

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Why We Collect It

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry. We will not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.

How We Store It

Your data is stored securely and only retained for as long as necessary to deal with your enquiry. We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Third-Party Services

This website does not currently use analytics, advertising or third-party tracking services. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. This policy was last updated in May 2025.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at hello@imogensvision.co.uk.
